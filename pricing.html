Material Science

Marine-Grade Engineering
For the Modern Home

At Florence, we replaced the fragile standards of the past. Traditional MDF and wood vanities are porous—they breathe, they swell, and eventually, they rot. Our proprietary High-Density PVC is a non-porous polymer engineered to remain structurally inert.

  • Termite & Pest Immunity

    Synthetic composition makes it naturally unattractive to pests, ensuring zero internal damage.

  • Eco-Longevity

    By creating products that last decades rather than years, we significantly reduce landfill waste.
