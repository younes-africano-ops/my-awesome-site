Crafting Durability for the Modern Home

Standard bathrooms suffer from a fundamental flaw: water and wood do not mix. Moisture leads to swelling, mold, and eventual structural failure. At Florence, we solved this by utilizing high-density PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) technology.

Florence PVC

100% Waterproof. Structural integrity for 20+ years. Termite-proof. Maintenance-free finish.

Our products are engineered for the smart homeowner who values longevity. By choosing Florence, you are investing in a space that remains as crisp and elegant on day 5,000 as it was on day one.
